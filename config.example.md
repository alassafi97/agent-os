### Scoring (1-5)
- **5 — Perfect fit:** SaaS or services company, 20-100 employees, $2-20M revenue, no outbound, founder wants predictable pipeline, $10K+ ACV

- **2 — Cold:** Under 10 employees, or already has a working outbound system
- **1 — No fit:** Lifestyle business with no growth ambition, or direct competitor
